Pedal Detroit

Pedal Detroit was my team's final project at Grand Circus. The purpose of Pedal Detroit was to allow people to choose premade bike routes around the City of Detroit or make their own routes. The project was required to be built using Angular JS with at least two directives, use at least one API, and also include some design aspects. For this project, we used two API's - OpenWeatherMap and Google Maps.
